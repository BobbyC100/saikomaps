Variant A — Compact Single Row

Everything on one meta row: category · neighborhood · price · status dot. Info section drops from ~100px to ~55px. Minimal, scannable.

Variant B — Compact + Creator Note

Same compressed meta row as A, but adds the creator's note below a thin divider. The note fills the space with actual useful content instead of padding. Same total card height, way more information.

Photo on the left, all info on the right. Most space-efficient layout. Cards take up less vertical space so you can see more places at once. Works well for dense guides with 20+ places.
